Portfolio · Equity Research Models

Integrated financial models and equity research notes built from scratch using public filings. Each company gets the same treatment: a 12-tab Excel model with live formulas, a professional equity research note, and a Python validation script that cross-checks every input. Designed to replicate the work of a junior analyst at an investment bank or Big 4 advisory firm.

3-statement model DCF valuation Trading comps IFRS 16 Scenario analysis Python + openpyxl

Tesco PLC (TSCO LN)

EQUITY RESEARCH 01 · April 2026

Sector: UK Grocery / Consumer Staples

Thesis: Tesco is the UK's largest grocer with roughly 27% market share. The 12-tab integrated model covers revenue (UK & ROI + Central Europe), EBITDA, full PP&E and ROU asset rollforwards, IFRS 16 lease liability schedules, a debt rollforward, dividend and buyback modelling, a DCF with mid-year convention and Gordon Growth terminal value, and a 6-peer trading comps table.

Diageo PLC (DGE LN)

EQUITY RESEARCH 02 · April 2026

Sector: Global Spirits / Consumer Staples

Thesis: Diageo is the world's largest spirits company, with brands including Johnnie Walker, Tanqueray, Guinness, and Don Julio. The model uses the same 12-tab architecture but adapted for spirits: revenue segmented into North America and International, much higher EBITDA margins (~35% vs ~9.5%), a large intangible asset base, and inventory where DIO runs at 160–170 days because spirits must age in barrels.
