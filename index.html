About This Series

Why one-pagers?

Professional investment research demands extreme concision. Each memo must communicate a complete investment thesis — data, risks, and a clear stance — on a single page. This mirrors the format used in asset management morning notes, investment committee briefs, and private equity screening memos.

Methodology

  • Identify a sector, macro theme, or structural trend
  • Form a falsifiable thesis with data anchors
  • Identify the key constraint or transmission mechanism
  • Quantify the mispricing or opportunity
  • Articulate the catalyst and exit condition
  • Risk-weight the case honestly

Each memo is accompanied by a supporting Excel workbook containing the underlying data tables and charts used in the analysis — available as a secondary download for those who want to examine the data directly.
