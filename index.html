Biotech & Life Sciences: Valuation Reset Meets the Rate Cycle

The XBI trades 27% below its 10-year mean EV/Sales after a 66% drawdown from 2021 to 2022. Rising real yields caused the de-rating by compressing long-duration cash flows. About This Series

Why short memos?

Professional investment research demands concision without sacrificing rigour. Each memo communicates a complete investment thesis — the data, the risks, and a clear stance — in the format used for buy-side morning notes, investment committee briefs, and private equity screening memos. Short enough to read in five minutes. Substantive enough to stand behind.

Methodology

Each memo follows the same framework:

  • Identify a sector, macro theme, or market trend
  • Articulate the catalyst and exit condition
  • Form a falsifiable thesis with data anchors
  • Risk-weight the case honestly
  • Identify the key constraint or transmission mechanism
  • Quantify the mispricing or opportunity

Every memo is backed by a supporting Excel workbook containing the underlying data, OLS regression outputs, scenario models, and charts — all fully auditable with a dedicated Audit Log tab showing exactly how each figure was derived.
